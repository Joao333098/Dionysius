Dionysus - The God of Wine and Celebration

Dionysus

  • Dionysus, the son of Zeus and Semele, is the Greek god of wine, celebration, and fertility. He embodies the joy of life, liberation, and transformation. His festivals, known as Bacchanalia, were characterized by ecstatic dances, music, and wine.
  • Dionysus symbolizes rebirth, having been “born” twice—once from the mortal Semele and again after being saved by Zeus. He is often depicted with vines and a wine cup, representing abundance, indulgence, and fertility.

Additional Information

  • Family members: Zeus and Semele
